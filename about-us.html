A little history about us

FIEK Kino was developed by two college students who were really big movie fans in the year 2021. What made them start this is because their city didn't have a cinema for almost 30 years.

They were intrigued by the idea of actually creating a place for their city and they actually started really small by only having one 150 seated auditorium, but they had bigger plans to open more than 5 auditoriums in bigger capacity.

The Cinema or the "Kino" as they call it in the city was actually a really big success where auditoriums were always fully booked. The movies that they show range from action to dramas to the Hollywood's newest additions.
